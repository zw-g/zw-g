# Style Guide — Based on Alien GIF Analysis

## Art Style
**Cute hand-drawn doodle** — indie webcomic / Tumblr-style casual illustration
- NOT sci-fi, NOT cyberpunk, NOT "hacker terminal"
- Think: sticker art, notebook doodle, wholesome internet culture

## Key Visual Characteristics
- **Thick, slightly imperfect black outlines** — hand-drawn feel
- **Flat color fills** — no gradients, no shadows, no 3D
- **Minimal detail** — simplified forms
- **Small whimsical decorations** — daisies, scattered dots, stars
- **Clean white/light background** — NOT dark

## Color Palette (extracted from alien GIF)
| Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| Mint/Light Green | #90EE90 | Alien skin, primary accent |
| Black | #000000 | Outlines, text, eyes |
| Dark Navy | #2D2D3D | Baseball cap, secondary dark |
| Off-White/Cream | #F5E6D0 | T-shirt, warm accent |
| White | #FFFFFF | Background, highlights |
| Yellow | #FFD700 | Flower centers, small accents |

## What This Means for Components
- **Headers/Footers**: Soft, light colors — NOT dark gradients
- **Stats/Graphs**: Mint green (#90EE90) as primary chart color on white/light bg
- **Weather Globe**: Should look like a cute doodle Earth, NOT realistic 3D
- **Icons**: Simple, clean — match the minimal hand-drawn feel
- **Text animations**: Soft, gentle — NOT flashy/techy
- **Overall vibe**: Friendly, approachable, slightly quirky — like the alien itself
